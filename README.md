Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes e-commerce sales performance (2019–2022) to uncover trends, track key business metrics, and enable data-driven decision-making.
The dashboard provides a clear view of sales, profit, and quantity performance across customer segments, categories, and regions.

Objective

To analyze and visualize sales performance trends, provide actionable insights, and track key metrics such as sales revenue, profit, and quantity sold — helping businesses improve strategy and growth.

Key Metrics Tracked

Total Sales Revenue: Overall income generated during the analysis period.
Total Profit: Net profitability of sales transactions.
Total Quantity Sold: Total product units sold.
Customer Segment-wise Sales: Performance analysis by different customer segments.
Category-wise Sales: Comparison of product categories over time.
Regional Sales Details: Drill-down view of sales performance across regions, states, and cities.

Dashboard Features

Interactive Filters & Slicers: Dynamic filtering by profit, quantity, and sales.
Area Chart: Customer segment–wise performance trends over time.
Line Chart: Month-wise sales trends based on product category.
KPI Cards: Highlights Total Sales, Total Profit, and Total Quantity (auto-updates with selections).
Details Page: Deep-dive view showing order-level details with slicer-based interactivity.
Navigation Button: Quick access to detailed insights from the main dashboard.

Insights Discovered

Q4 consistently shows peak performance across all customer segments.
West Region generated the highest profit, followed by East and South regions.
Customer interaction with charts dynamically updates KPIs, enabling real-time comparative analysis.
The dashboard helps identify high-performing products, customer segments, and regions for strategic focus.
